## What happened

Continued the search rewrite on `wt/search-index`. The inverted index now builds from
all ~800 recipes in about two seconds, and substring search is fully replaced behind
the same API — the frontend didn't need to change at all.

Hit ranking next and stalled: title matches and body matches currently score the same,
so searching "lemon" buries *Lemon Tart* under forty recipes that merely mention lemon
zest. We decided title weight is the next session's first task rather than tuning it
tired tonight.

## Decisions

- **Index rebuilds on write, not on a timer.** Recipe volume is low; rebuilding on
  every save is simpler than cache invalidation and costs nothing at this scale.
  Revisit only if saves get slow.
- Deferred fuzzy matching entirely — it's a Horizon item, not part of this rewrite.
